Adrienne “AK” Kelly, MS, MCST

Adrienne “AK” Kelly is more than a chef — she is a visionary, healer, and cultural architect redefining nourishment.

With a Master of Science in Medical Cannabis Science & Therapeutics, AK brings academic precision and real-world knowledge into a space where wellness, creativity, and culture meet.

Her experiences are designed not only to feed the body, but to elevate the mind, reconnect people, and create unforgettable shared moments.

Beyond the kitchen, she is also a writer and creative visionary using storytelling to inspire transformation and healing.
